About JMGOLDBUYERS

Your Trusted Partner in Gold Transactions

Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the gold buying industry in Guwahati, JMGOLDBUYERS has been serving the community with integrity and excellence for over a decade. What started as a small venture has grown into one of the most trusted names in gold trading across Assam.

Our journey began when Mr. Janak Mahanto, with his extensive knowledge of precious metals and deep understanding of customer needs, decided to create a business that prioritizes transparency, fairness, and customer satisfaction above everything else.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of customers who trust us with their most precious possessions, knowing they will receive honest evaluations and the best market rates.

Meet Our Founder

Mr. Janak Mahanto

With over 15 years of experience in the precious metals industry, Mr. Janak Mahanto brings unparalleled expertise and passion to JMGOLDBUYERS. His deep understanding of gold markets, combined with a commitment to ethical business practices, has made him a respected figure in Guwahati's gold trading community.

Mr. Mahanto believes that every customer deserves honest treatment and fair pricing. His hands-on approach ensures that every transaction meets the highest standards of quality and transparency.

"Our customers' trust is our most valuable asset. We work every day to maintain and strengthen that trust through honest dealings and exceptional service." - Janak Mahanto
